TOP TRENDING COURSES

We offer a wide and flexible range of courses to meet your needs.

Diploma in Financial Market (DIFM)

ByAdmin

InYOURTRADER

DIFM is a 6 month industry oriented course specially designed for those who want to make their career in financial institutions , broking houses, research houses, depository houses, want to work as a arbitrager, executive or manager, investor or trader.

Advance Market Maker (AMM)

ByAdmin

InYOURTRADER

AMM is the best-advanced fast-track course designed for stock market traders. AMM covers all basic to advance curriculum a trader requirement in stock market trading in Futures, Capital, Commodities, Currency & Technical Analysis.

Advance Smart Trader (AST)

ByAdmin

Inyourtrader

AST is specially designed for those who want to become full-time successful trader & investor. This course cover NSE NCFM and SEBI NISM certifications of capital market, Derivatives market , Commodities market & Currency markets.

Professional Research Analyst (PRA)

ByAdmin

InYOURTRADER

Professional Research Analyst is a short term course with 100% Job placement assistance in broking houses, financial institutions and banking industry.
